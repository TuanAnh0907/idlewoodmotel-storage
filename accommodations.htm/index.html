Quiet country setting!

The Idlewood offers reasonable rates, clean, comfortable rooms, and plenty of parking. All rooms are air conditioned. All rooms feature ground level entrance with parking right outside your room. We have daily maid service, cable TV, and a beautiful outdoor pool area. Each room has a phone and local calls are free. Nonsmoking rooms are available.

Efficiency apartments and long term rates are also available. We accept all major credit cards.

With our convenient location 1/2 mile from downtown Franklin and 1/4 mile from the Venango Regional Airport, we are the perfect choice for cost effective, comfortable accommodations in the Venango County Area!
