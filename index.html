The Idlewood is an 18 unit family owned and operated motel, doing business at this location for over 60 years. The motel is located in a quiet wooded area only minutes from downtown Franklin. Our rooms are updated and refreshed on a regular basis.

Whether you are in the area for business or pleasure, please give us a try. Come and see why we are proud of our area. Our staff will make your stay a pleasant one.
